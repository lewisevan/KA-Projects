Tips on Choosing a Starting Color

  • Use What You Have. if you have an image or logo you want to include, start with one of those colors.
  • Think about Your Target Audience. think about who will be using your website and how you want them to feel. (happy, sad, excited, satisfied, taken care of, etc.)
  • Don't Default to Stereotypes. try to avoid clich√©s, it will increase your credibility. a website intended for a female audience doesn't have to be pink, and a website for an older audience can use fun colors too!
  • Brainstorm. if you're stuck, write down a list of words that you can associate with your topic. What colors come to mind?
